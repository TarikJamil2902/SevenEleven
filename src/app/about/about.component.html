Company Overview

Established in 2015, SevenEleven Engineering & Technical Services has emerged as a leading provider of comprehensive engineering and technical solutions in Bangladesh.   • Electrical Systems & Automation
  • Industrial Maintenance & Support
  • Technical Consultancy

At SevenEleven, we combine cutting-edge technology with engineering excellence to provide reliable, efficient, and sustainable solutions. Our team of certified professionals ensures that every project adheres to the highest industry standards while maintaining a strong focus on safety, quality, and client satisfaction.
